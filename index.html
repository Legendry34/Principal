Inter school debate comepetition

Inter-school debate competitions are an excellent platform for students to showcase their debating skills and enhance their knowledge on various topics. These competitions provide an opportunity for students to engage in healthy discussions and develop their critical thinking abilities.

Inter school sports comepetition

Interschool competitions can foster long-term relationships between students if they have similar goals or objectives in mind and if they have sufficient sportsmanship about it. Networking is a very important aspect when it comes to higher studies which will help them in the long run.
